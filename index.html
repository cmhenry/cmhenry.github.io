Colin M. Henry
I'm a political scientist working on problems at the intersection of political violence and digital communication technologies. I use computational methods, network analysis, and digital ethnography to answer questions about political extremism, social media platforms, community structure, and our relationship to technology. My dissertation asks: what are the structural sources of political extremism in online communities?
Current status: I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Political Science at Vanderbilt University. Expected completion 8/23.
